About MyNorfolk

This dashboard is a demo of Quarto dashboards and is not affiliated with the city of Norfolk.

MyNorfolk is a service that allows residents of the City of Norfolk to request services online from their computer or mobile device. The data used in this dashboard is available on the Norfolk Open Data Portal.
